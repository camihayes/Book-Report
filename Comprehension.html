Comprehension

Settings

  • This story takes place in Ancient Egypt. The first setting is a place called Ahkmin. This is where Nefertiti lives. It includes a town and her mansion. Her mansion has a shrine to the god Isis in the front yard and many rooms in the house itself. The town has shops and other houses. The next setting is Abydos. Abydos has many shrines, temples, obelisks, statues and tons of wildlife. It also includes Amun's house, but this is all we are shown.


  • The final and largest setting is Thebes. This is basically a kingdom. There are areas for banquets, different quarters for men and women, and the Great Palace. Pharoah's quarters are seperated from the rest. As we have seen, it is easy to get lost without a guide. There is also a prison, as we have seen towards the end. Every setting is surrounded by water. Overall, the setting in The Sphinx's Princess is very ancient and beautiful.
