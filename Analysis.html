Analysis

Explain the Title

I think the author chose the title “Sphinx Princess” because Nefertiti used to have nightmares. In said nightmares she would be playing in her yard, but the ground turns to sand and devours her house, then lions come out and come towards her. The lions' faces become human, and right before they kill her she wakes up screaming. Her father then prays to Isis to cast away her nightmares. Now, in her dreams she rules the sphinxes, and rides them to the highest skies and farthest lands. I think since she rules them, she is the sphinx's princess.
